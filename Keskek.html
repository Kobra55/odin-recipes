Keskek

Description

Keskek is a traditional turkish dish. It is made of wheat and is similar to wheat poridge. It is served warm and with a tomato paprika Butter. It is a very simple dish but if you want to cook it you have to have time, because it is usually prepared a day earlier.

Keskek recipe

Ingredients

    For the wheat

  • 300 g wheat
  • 3 Liter water
  • 200 g Butter
  • salt

    • For the sauce

  • butter
  • 3 ts of Butter
  • Tomato paste
  • Paprika powder

Steps

    Preaperation of the wheat

  1. Let the wheat soak in water for 24 hours. After that strain the water and pour in 2 Liters of new water. Dont't forget the salt and evtl. pepper. Let it cook for aother 2 hours. Finished with that you can give the 200 gramms of Butter to the wheat and mash the wheat.

    2. Preperation of the sauce

  2. Give the Butterin to a heated pan and let it melt. Once melted give in the ingredients such as, tomato paste and the paprika powder and stir it for a few minutes.

    3. Serve

  3. Now place the wheat on a Plate and pour the sauce over the wheat.
