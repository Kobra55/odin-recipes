Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

    For the yeast dough

  • 500 g wheat flour
  • 300 ml water
  • 2 Tsp salt
  • 5 g of fresh yeast

    • For the sauce

  • 1 can of Pizza tomatos
  • salt and pepper
  • a little bit of sugar
  • oregano
  • basil

Steps

    Preperation ot the dough

  1. First sift the flour with the salt and pour it into a dough mixer or a bread maker with a dough function. Now dissolve the yeast in the lukewarm water and then add it to the flour-salt mixture

    2. Then knead the ingredients into an elastic, homogeneous dough for 20 minutes. Note: An electric aid is almost mandatory here; if necessary, a hand mixer with a dough hook will also work. Please don't knead desperately with your bare hand, the dough simply won't achieve the desired softness and consistency.

    After kneading, cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for two hours. Then divide into four pieces of 200 g each, form them into balls and cover again and let rise for another six hours.

    Preperation of the sauce

  2. For the tomato sauce, simply puree a can of peeled tomatoes with (lots of) garlic, salt, pepper , a little sugar, basil and oregano, do not boil.

    3. Preparing the Pizza

  3. Then apply the tomato sauce to the middle of the pizza and spread it over the entire pizza in circular movements with the trowel. Don't apply too much! A good pizza shouldn't be drowned in tomato sauce. If individual areas of the dough don't get any sauce, it doesn't matter at all. Better a little less than a little more.

    4. Preheat the oven to maximum! Place the dough on the hot baking tray (this is easier with baking paper) and after just 3 - 4 minutes the pizza is ready and can be enjoyed.
